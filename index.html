Description

This innovative web application leverages extensive historical data analysis to forecast the potential severity of earthquakes, fires, and tornadoes. By drawing insights from past occurrences, the platform employs powerfull tools like machine learning and natural langauge processing to predict and assess the likelihood of future natural disasters. Users can gain a comprehensive understanding of the historical patterns and factors influencing the occurrence and intensity of these events, enabling proactive measures to mitigate their impact.
